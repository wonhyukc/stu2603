## Learning Objectives / 학습 목표

1. Understand the AI-Native Computing Thinking approach and the Flipped Learning methodology.
   AI-Native 컴퓨팅 사고 및 Flipped Learning(거꾸로 수업) 방식을 이해한다.
2. Grasp business email principles and the engineer-style questioning method.
   비즈니스 이메일 원칙과 엔지니어식 질문법의 기본기를 파악한다.

   문서와 브라우저의 관계를 쉬운 비유로 통해 웹의 기본 원리를 이해한다.

## Time Allocation & Sequence / 시간 배분 및 진행 순서

### Theory Block / 이론 블록 (75 min = 25 min × 3 sections)

| Section | Time / 시간 | Topic / 주제 | Key Points / 핵심 포인트 |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Section 1** | 25 min | Web History & Course Intro / 웹 역사와 강의 소개 | What is the web (analogy), brief web history, semester roadmap, Flipped Learning |
| **Section 2** | 25 min | Professional Communication / 프로페셔널 커뮤니케이션 | Business email 5 principles, engineer-style questioning, eCampus usage |
| **Section 3** | 25 min | AI Partner & Dev Tools / AI 파트너와 개발 도구 | StackBlitz intro, GitHub overview, AI as coding partner, AI critique mindset, Micro-Assignment format |

### Hands-on Block / 조작 블록 (75 min)

Instructor mirrors on projector; students follow along on their own browsers.
강사는 빔 프로젝터로 화면 공유하며 학생이 직접 따라 하도록 유도한다.

1. **StackBlitz Access & First HTML Tag / StackBlitz 접속 및 첫 HTML 태그 체험** (25 min)
   - Open [stackblitz.com](https://stackblitz.com) → Create new Static project → Type first `<h1>` tag → Live preview.

2. **GitHub Account Creation & Repository Setup / GitHub 계정 생성 및 저장소 세팅** (25 min)
   - Sign up at [github.com](https://github.com) → Create private repository (`web-assignments` or similar) → **Invite instructor `wonhyukc` as Collaborator** (mandatory).

3. **eCampus Check + AI Account Creation / eCampus 확인 + AI 계정 생성** (25 min)
   - Verify this course appears in eCampus enrollment list.
   - Create or log in to **ChatGPT or Google Gemini** account.
   - Ask AI: "What is HTML?" — read the answer critically.

### AI Review & Wrap-up Block / AI리뷰·마무리 블록 (30 min)

#### Step 1: Share & Critique AI Answers (15 min) / AI 답변 공유 및 비평

Ask 2–3 students to read their AI response aloud. Lead a short discussion:
2~3명을 지목해 AI 답변을 큰 소리로 읽게 하고 토의를 이끈다.

- "Was anything the AI said different from what we covered today?"
- "Did the AI admit it might be wrong? How did it phrase that?"
- "What part was hardest to understand?"

**Instructor note**: This is the first training session for students to question AI answers. The goal is not to find errors under pressure, but to build the habit of healthy skepticism.
강사 포인트: 틀린 부분을 찾아야 한다는 압박보다 "AI도 틀릴 수 있다"는 감각을 심어주는 것이 이 시간의 목적이다.

#### Step 2: Assignment Briefing (10 min) / 과제 안내

- Open the Week 1 submission box on eCampus and show it on screen.
- Briefly explain both assignments from `handout.md`:
  - **Assignment A**: Self-introduction email to instructor — reinforce business email principles.
  - **Assignment B**: Create `week01.md` in GitHub repo with 3 verification items (StackBlitz URL / GitHub profile URL / AI conversation text), then submit the GitHub file link on eCampus.
- **Emphasize**: Instructor (`wonhyukc`) must be invited as Collaborator, otherwise the submission cannot be accessed → **0 points**.

#### Step 3: Preview & Q&A (5 min) / 예고 및 마무리 Q&A

- Next week (wb02) topic: HTML Basics — building an online business card with `<h1>`, `<p>`, `<img>`.
- Remind students to watch the wb02 video on eCampus before the next class.
- Open Q&A.

## Instructor Notes / 강사 유의 사항

- Week 1 is more about explaining **how this course works** than teaching web code. Keep the focus on Flipped Learning, AI-Native thinking, and professional communication — not HTML syntax.
  1주차는 HTML 문법보다 **수업 방식 설명**이 우선이다. Flipped Learning과 AI 파트너 개념 정착에 집중한다.
- GitHub account creation may be slow due to email verification delays. Tell students it is fine to finish by next week if they cannot complete it today.
  GitHub 인증 메일이 오래 걸릴 수 있다. 오늘 안 되면 다음 주까지 완료하도록 안내한다.
- Expect the question: "Is this class hard if I can't code?" Answer: "This class is not about memorizing code. AI writes the code; you learn to judge whether it is correct."
  "코딩 못 하면 이 수업 어렵나요?" 질문 예상. "AI가 코드를 짜고, 여러분은 그게 맞는지 판단하는 능력을 키웁니다."라고 답한다.
- The business email assignment is not just a first task — it sets the **communication standard for the entire semester**.
  비즈니스 이메일 과제는 단순한 첫 과제가 아니라 한 학기 내내 쓸 소통 기준이다.
