# Week 6 Homework Peer Evaluation Rubric

## 📌 Grading Instructions
- **Objective Evaluation**: Please evaluate your peer's submission based on the objective criteria below. Each "Yes" is worth 1 point.
- **Total Score**: Max 8 points.

---

| No. | Evaluation Criteria | Points |
|:---:|:---|:---:|
| **1** | **On-time Submission**: Did they submit the assignment within the deadline? | **5 points** |
| **2** | **Card Border**: Does the `.card` element have a visible **border**? | (Yes 1 / No 0) |
| **3** | **Card Padding**: Is there internal **padding** inside the `.card` element? | (Yes 1 / No 0) |
| **4** | **Element Margin**: Are elements (like buttons or p tags) separated using **margin**? | (Yes 1 / No 0) |
| **5** | **Data Import**: Is the Kaggle Titanic dataset prominently imported into the Google Sheet? | (Yes 1 / No 0) |
| **6** | **Frozen Row**: Is the **first row** of the Google Sheet **frozen**? | (Yes 1 / No 0) |
| **7** | **Data Sorting**: Is the dataset explicitly **sorted** by a column (e.g., Fare or Age)? | (Yes 1 / No 0) |
| **8** | **Conditional Formatting**: Is a **Color Scale** applied to a numerical column? | (Yes 1 / No 0) |
| **9** | **Pivot Table**: Is there a functional **Pivot Table** sheet analyzing the dataset? | (Yes 1 / No 0) |

---

## 💡 Evaluator's Note
- **Objectivity First**: Grade based strictly on the individual conditions above, regardless of the complexity or aesthetic design.
- **Access Check**: If any links (GitHub repo or Google Sheet) are inaccessible due to permissions or errors, the total score is **0**.
